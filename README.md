# Pricing-Strategy-Analysis-Dashboard
# 💰 Pricing Strategy & Revenue Optimization Dashboard

A data-driven **Power BI project** that analyzes product pricing, revenue performance, customer demand, and regional sales trends. This project focuses on identifying profitable price ranges, top-performing products, and revenue optimization opportunities using key metrics such as total revenue, units sold, orders, average price, and revenue per order.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes product sales and pricing data to answer key business questions:

- Which price range generates the highest revenue?
- Which products perform best by revenue, units sold, and orders?
- How does product price affect customer demand and sales volume?
- Which countries or regions contribute the most revenue?
- Which products or price ranges should be reviewed for better pricing strategy?

The insights are visualized through an interactive **Power BI dashboard**.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Power BI** → Dashboard design and data visualization
- **Power Query** → Data cleaning and transformation
- **DAX** → KPI measures and calculated metrics
- **Excel** → Source dataset and initial data preparation

## 📈 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Summary

Provides a high-level overview of business performance using KPIs such as total revenue, total customers, average price, revenue per order, total units sold, and total orders.

This page also compares revenue and units sold across different price ranges and countries.

### 2. Sales vs Price Analysis

Analyzes the relationship between product price, revenue, and units sold.

This page helps understand how pricing affects customer demand and highlights the top 10 products by revenue and units sold.

### 3. Product Performance Analysis

Compares product-level performance based on orders, revenue, units sold, and average price.

This page helps identify best-selling products, high-revenue products, and products that may need pricing review.

### 4. Country / Region Analysis

Shows revenue, orders, units sold, and average price by country or region.

This page helps identify high-performing markets and regions with higher average selling prices.

### 5. Pricing Recommendations & Key Insights

Summarizes the major findings from the dashboard and provides recommendations for improving pricing strategy and revenue performance.

---

## 🔍 Dashboard Insights

- **Medium-priced products** generated the highest revenue and unit sales, showing strong customer demand for affordable mid-range pricing.
- **Low-priced products** drove high sales volume but did not generate the highest overall revenue.
- **Premium-priced products** had higher average prices but lower sales volume, indicating a need for pricing or positioning review.
- Revenue was concentrated among a small number of top-performing products.
- The **United Kingdom** contributed the majority of revenue, orders, and units sold.
- Some international markets showed higher average selling prices, creating opportunities for region-specific pricing strategies.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Maintain competitive pricing for high-volume products.
- Test moderate price increases on strong-selling products.
- Bundle low-volume products with bestselling products.
- Use region-specific pricing strategies.
- Review premium products with weak sales volume.
- Focus marketing and promotional efforts on top revenue-generating products.
